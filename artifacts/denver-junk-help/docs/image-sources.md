## Image Direction for Launch

Replace placeholders with **royalty-free, commercial-use licensed** images from sources like:
- [Unsplash](https://unsplash.com) (free, commercial use with attribution)
- [Pexels](https://pexels.com) (free, commercial use)
- [Adobe Stock](https://stock.adobe.com) (paid, broad commercial license)
- [Shutterstock](https://shutterstock.com) (paid, broad commercial license)

### Preferred Subject Matter
- Clean garage or basement cleared for pickup
- Furniture staged near a curb or in a room ready for removal
- Appliance (fridge, washer) being moved or staged
- Curbside bulky items — neutral, no logos
- Tidy home cleanout scene with boxes/items piled neatly
- Neutral loading or removal context with no visible company branding

### Avoid
- Branded trucks or uniforms implying Denver Junk Help operates the service
- AI-generated photos with obvious artifacts
- Extreme hoarder or distressing content as hero visuals
- Stock call-center or customer service imagery
- Denver landmark clichés unless very natural in context
