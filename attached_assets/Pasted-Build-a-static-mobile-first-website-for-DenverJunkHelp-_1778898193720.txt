Build a static, mobile-first website for DenverJunkHelp.com.

Project name:
Denver Junk Help

Purpose:
A clean, trustworthy local quote and referral site for junk removal in the Denver area.
This site should feel like a serious local intake desk for junk-removal service.
It should NOT claim to be the hauling company.
It should sound close to the work and operationally helpful, but accurate about its role.

Core positioning:
“Denver Junk Help is a local quote and referral website that helps people in Denver request junk-removal service from a trusted local provider.”

Voice and tone:
- Plainspoken
- Helpful
- Local
- Calm
- Trustworthy
- Not gimmicky
- Not salesy
- Not detached
- Not corporate-franchise hype
- No fake urgency
- No “#1 best in Denver”
- No fake claims about crews, trucks, licensing, or insurance

Public pages for V1:
1. Home
2. Furniture Removal
3. Appliance Removal
4. Cleanouts
5. Pricing & FAQ
6. Denver Large Item Pickup vs Private Junk Removal
7. Contact / Quote Request
8. Privacy Policy
9. Terms of Use

Design direction:
- Warm off-white / light neutral background
- Dark charcoal text
- Deep green or navy accent
- One muted warm accent color
- Modern sans-serif typography
- Large readable headings
- Clean spacing
- Subtle borders/cards
- Very little animation
- Realistic home-service feel
- No cartoon trucks
- No mascot branding
- No autoplay video
- No popups
- No testimonial carousel
- No fake review badges
- No fake address
- No fake team photos

Mobile behavior:
- Sticky bottom CTA bar on mobile with only:
  - Call
  - Request Quote
- All important copy must be present on mobile, not hidden away
- Fast load, simple JS

Required disclosures:
Homepage/body disclosure:
“Denver Junk Help is a local quote and referral website. We help people in Denver request junk-removal service from a trusted local provider. We do not perform hauling or disposal services ourselves.”

Form consent text:
“By submitting this form, you agree that Denver Junk Help may share your request details with a local junk-removal provider so they can contact you about service, pricing, and availability.”

Footer disclosure:
“Denver Junk Help is not a junk-removal company and does not operate removal trucks. Service availability, final pricing, and job completion are handled by the provider that responds to your request.”
